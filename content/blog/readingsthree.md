## ChatGPT is a Blurry JPEG of the Web - Ted Chiang, 2023

# This idea of lossless versus lossy.

“If the restored file is identical to the original, then the compression process is described as lossless: no information has been discarded.”

Judging the rooms and thinking of them as similiar caused the digital image to show all rooms the same size. You could also describe the computer as processing in patterns, and this is one of its the pitfalls. For instance, a strange thing happened to me once. I asked ChatGPT to analyze a story and give critique. I did a few of the same query. Once, after the 4th query say, the analysis shockingly actually made up stuff that wasn’t in the story! Events that happened, but still kept the essential gist of the characters. A hallucination as defined in the article. The “common technique used by lossy compression algorithms is interpolation”.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/legenddoros/assets/refs/heads/main/web-design-blog-legend/images/resistcolor.jpg">

I've used AI to critique my writing, and similiarly when asked for best lines or what to edit, after several repeat queries the same line will sometimes eventually be attributed as both “best” and “needed to edit or problematic “.
